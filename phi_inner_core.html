Inner φ-Core · Mercury–Venus–Earth–Mars

Inner φ-Core describes the inner Solar-System floor in φ-geometry: the region dominated by Mercury, Venus, Earth and Mars. In the public edition, only conceptual description and selected static visuals are published.

The full computational model (ephemeris pipelines, φ-tests and resonance analysis) is maintained in private ORION Engine repositories.
