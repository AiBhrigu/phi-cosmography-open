X-Domain · φ-Search Shell

X-Domain is the outer φ-shell of the Solar System atlas: the region beyond Neptune where long-period and detached objects form the search space for Planet X–type candidates. This public page shows only a conceptual map and qualitative structure. All resonance engines, filters and discovery algorithms stay private inside ORION.

  • Inner φ-Core — the inner Solar-System floor (Mercury–Mars).
  • φ-Floors — Jupiter–Saturn and Uranus–Neptune floors that define the outer architecture of the giant system.
  • Helion φ⁵ — the bridge node that connects φ-Floors to the X-domain search shell.
